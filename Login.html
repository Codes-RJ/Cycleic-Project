About Us

Welcome to Cycleic — a platform built for the cycling community, by the cycling community. We are a Mumbai-based startup that began with a simple mission: to make it easier, safer, and more convenient to buy and sell pre-owned bicycles and cycling accessories online.

Our journey started in 2019 when we were searching for a good-quality second-hand bike and realized how unorganized and scattered the existing options were. From unreliable listings on social media to poor product descriptions and a lack of trust, the experience was far from smooth. That frustration sparked an idea — why not build a dedicated online marketplace for cycles, just like cars and motorbikes have?

In 2020, Cycleic was born. Since then, we've been committed to creating a trusted ecosystem where buyers and sellers from across India can connect, communicate, and complete deals with confidence. Whether you're looking to sell your first kid's bicycle or upgrade to a high-performance mountain bike, we're here to simplify the process.

At Cycleic, we believe cycling is more than just a mode of transport — it's a lifestyle, a fitness journey, and a path to sustainability. That's why we focus not just on selling bikes, but on building a culture around cycling. We aim to promote eco-friendly transportation, healthier living, and a stronger community of riders.

Our platform is user-friendly, mobile-optimized, and designed with trust and transparency in mind. Every product listing goes through a basic verification process before it goes live, and we provide helpful tools to guide buyers and sellers every step of the way.

From tricycles and city commuters to road racers and MTB beasts — we support all types of bicycles. We also help sellers reach a wider audience by overcoming geographical limits that might otherwise prevent a sale.

Looking ahead, we plan to introduce features like doorstep delivery, secure payment options, and more community-driven features such as rider reviews, cycling blogs, and local ride events. We're not just building a marketplace — we're building a movement.

Thank you for being a part of the Cycleic family. Whether you're a seasoned rider or just getting started, we're excited to be part of your journey on two wheels. Together, let's ride toward a cleaner, healthier, and more connected future.
